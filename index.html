Description

Located in a 13th-century Augustinian monastery — Augustine, A Luxury Collection Hotel, Prague, boasts design inspired by early 20th-century Czech cubism. Discover lavish rooms with impeccable service and the latest in modern conveniences and amenities. Along with dining and spa services onsite, guests can enjoy attractions like Charles Bridge, Kampa Island and Old Town Square, which are within minutes of the hotel.
